## Editorial Target

The target is not a literal translation. The target is a tighter Dutch/Flemish-market adaptation for broad intelligent non-fiction readers: teachers, trainers, students, professionals, managers, parents, and policy-minded readers who are curious or worried about AI.

Recommended working title:

**Het Laatste Licht: Hoe we AI kunnen gebruiken zonder onszelf uit te besteden**
