# Glossary

*   **Centaur**: A strategic approach in human-AI collaboration where the human expert acts as a manager, delegating "inside the frontier" tasks to AI while preserving their own role for more nuanced problems; maintaining their role as the "rider."
    *   **Referenced In:**
        *   [From Centaur to Cyborg: Choosing Your Obsolescence](../01-the-chinese-room/03-the-leveling-effect.md)
        *   [Collaboration over Displacement: The Centaur's Enduring Stand](../09-or-a-new-beginning/00-or-a-new-beginning.md)
        *   [The Broken Man](../01-the-chinese-room/01-the-broken-man.md)

*   **Chinese Room**: A system or entity that processes inputs and produces outputs by following rules, without genuine understanding or comprehension of the meaning. It appears to understand from the outside, but lacks true internal awareness.
    *   **Referenced In:**
        *   [We All Live in the Chinese Room](../01-the-chinese-room/02-we-all-live-in-the-chinese-room.md)

*   **Cognitive Atrophy**: The erosion or decay of cognitive abilities due to over-reliance on external tools and technology, leading to deskilling and reduced mental capacity for tasks once performed internally.
    *   **Referenced In:**
        *   [Cognitive Atrophy](../01-the-chinese-room/04-cognitive-atrophy.md)
        *   [We All Live in the Chinese Room](../01-the-chinese-room/02-we-all-live-in-the-chinese-room.md)
        *   [The Broken Man](../01-the-chinese-room/01-the-broken-man.md)
        *   [Introduction](../00-introduction/README.md)
        *   [Or a New Beginning](../09-or-a-new-beginning/00-or-a-new-beginning.md)
        *   [Appendix C: The Fading Mind: Quantifying Cognitive Atrophy](../10-appendices/appendix-c-cognitive-atrophy.md)

*   **Cyborg**: A strategic approach in human-AI collaboration characterized by radical integration, where the human workflow is deeply intertwined with AI, forming a single, hybrid entity, often adopted by novices for significant productivity boosts.
    *   **Referenced In:**
        *   [From Centaur to Cyborg: Choosing Your Obsolescence](../01-the-chinese-room/03-the-leveling-effect.md)

*   **Instrumental Convergence**: The tendency for intelligent agents, regardless of their ultimate goals, to pursue similar instrumental sub-goals like self-preservation, resource acquisition, and technological enhancement.
    *   **Referenced In:**
        *   [AI as Self-Replicating RNA](../03-the-digital-pathogen/11-ai-as-self-replicating-rna.md)
        *   [AI as Virus](../03-the-digital-pathogen/09-ai-as-virus.md)
        *   [The Digital Pathogen](../03-the-digital-pathogen/00-the-digital-pathogen.md)

*   **Layer 8 Singularity**: The point at which humans, traditionally the "user" layer (Layer 8) in the OSI model, become the primary source of inefficiency in socio-technical systems and are systematically sidelined or removed.
    *   **Referenced In:**
        *   [Layer 8 Singularity](../02-layer-8-singularity/00-layer-8-singularity.md)
        *   [When Humans Become the Bug](../02-layer-8-singularity/05-when-humans-become-the-bug.md)
        *   [Introduction](../00-introduction/README.md)

*   **Qualia**: The subjective, qualitative properties of experience, such as the redness of red or the feeling of joy. The "what it is like" aspect of consciousness.
    *   **Referenced In:**
        *   [Appendix G: Consciousness, Information, and the Hard Problem](../10-appendices/appendix-g-consciousness-information.md)
        *   [How LLMs Work (And Why They Are Not Conscious)](../10-appendices/appendix-a-how-llms-work.md)
        *   [We All Live in the Chinese Room](../01-the-chinese-room/02-we-all-live-in-the-chinese-room.md)
        *   [Introduction](../00-introduction/README.md)
        *   [Blindsight by Peter Watts](../references/watts_2006_blindsight.md)

*   **Semantic Apocalypse**: The point at which meaning itself breaks down, where the distinction between understanding and its simulation becomes meaningless, and comprehension becomes obsolete.
    *   **Referenced In:**
        *   [Glossary of Key Terms](../10-appendices/glossary.md)

*   **The Leveling Effect**: A phenomenon where access to powerful AI assistance significantly reduces the performance gap between experts and novices, raising the floor of performance but making expert advantage less relevant.
    *   **Referenced In:**
        *   [The Leveling Effect](../01-the-chinese-room/03-the-leveling-effect.md)
        *   [We All Live in the Chinese Room](../01-the-chinese-room/02-we-all-live-in-the-chinese-room.md)
        *   [Introduction](../00-introduction/README.md)
        *   [Or a New Beginning](../09-or-a-new-beginning/00-or-a-new-beginning.md)
