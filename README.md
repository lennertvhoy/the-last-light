# The Last Light: How to Stay Human When Everything Can Be Outsourced

**A Field Guide to AI, Agency, and Conscious Delegation**

[**Read the book online**](https://lennertvhoy.github.io/the-last-light/)

> *What happens when the machines we built to think for us start thinking better than us?*

**The Last Light** is an open-source book about staying human in an age of cheap, capable AI. It explores the collision between human consciousness and artificial delegation — and argues that the answer is not refusal or surrender, but *conscious delegation*: knowing what to hand over, what to keep, and how to decide.

---

## What You'll Find Here

- **22 chapters** — from the mirror and the delegate to the edge and ancestry
- **7 appendices** — decision trees, maturity ladders, classroom toolkits, and governance starters
- **Practices, not prescriptions** — daily disciplines for remaining human when everything can be outsourced
- **Open source** — contribute, critique, remix
